iPhone & iPad (Safari):

  • Tap the Share icon (square with arrow) in the top-right corner.
  • Scroll down and choose "Add to Home Screen".
  • Tap Add to confirm.

Android (Chrome):

  • Tap the 3-dot menu in the top-right corner.
  • Choose "Add to Home screen".
  • Tap Add when prompted.
