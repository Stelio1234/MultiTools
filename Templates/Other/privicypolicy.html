Privacy Policy

What We Collect

  • We may collect anonymous analytics like device type, app usage, and general location (city-level).
  • If you log in using Google, we may access your name, email address, and profile picture — but only what's allowed by your Google account permissions.
  • No sensitive personal data (like passwords, banking info, or government IDs) is collected or stored by us directly.
  • Basic device information such as platform, screen size, timezone, and browser type is collected to help us understand how our tools are used and optimize compatibility.

How We Use Data

  • To improve our services and fix bugs.
  • To communicate with users when necessary (e.g. support or updates).
  • To comply with legal obligations (if the law ever comes knocking).

Cookies & Local Storage

  • This site may use cookies or local storage to save preferences or track simple interactions.
  • We do not sell or trade your data.

Third-Party Tools

  • We may use tools like Google Analytics, Firebase, or others to understand usage trends. They may collect anonymized data.
  • Links to other sites (like YouTube, TikTok, etc.) are not under our control, and their privacy practices apply separately.

Your Control

  • You can request deletion of any identifiable info by contacting us directly or use the built-in option on the Settings page to delete your account instantly.
  • Once deleted, your account and associated data are permanently removed from our system.
  • You are free to use the site anonymously unless specific features require login (like saving preferences or using advanced tools).

Authentication Services

  • We use Google Sign-In to make logging in fast and secure. This only shares basic profile info that you approve.
  • Google may collect usage data through their APIs; their Privacy Policy applies to that data.
  • We do not have access to your Google password or any private account settings.

Open Source Disclosure

  • This project is open source and hosted publicly on GitHub. Anyone can view the source code and contribute improvements.
  • We welcome transparency — feel free to review the code and verify how your data is handled.

Policy Updates

  • This policy may be updated at any time. We'll update the date below whenever that happens.
